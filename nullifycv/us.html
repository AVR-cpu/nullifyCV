How it works

Redact a resume in under 30 seconds

Upload a PDF or DOCX. Choose your redaction mode. Download the anonymized file. Zero bytes leave your device at any point.

1

Upload the resume

Drag a PDF or DOCX onto the tool. The file is read into browser memory only — it doesn't leave your device.

2

Choose a redaction mode

Standard PII, Bias Strip, Client Submission, or EEOC Blind Review. The EEOC mode removes school names, photos, and all location signals in addition to standard identifiers.

3

Processed in your browser

pdf.js and pdf-lib draw black redaction bars over PII positions directly in the original file — preserving fonts, layout, and formatting.

4

Download the redacted resume

The redacted file downloads directly to your device. The Blob URL is immediately revoked — nothing is retained or cached.
