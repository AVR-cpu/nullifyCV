For DPOs and legal teams
NullifyCV does not act as a data processor under GDPR Article 4(8). No personal data is transmitted to NullifyCV — all file processing occurs client-side in the user's browser. There is no data processing agreement required because no personal data is shared with us. The audit log generated by NullifyCV documents the redaction workflow for your own Records of Processing Activities (RoPA) under GDPR Article 30.
