Pro users can drop up to 200 PDF or DOCX files at once into the batch queue. The tool processes them sequentially in the browser, applies your saved redaction profile, and lets you download all redacted files as a ZIP — along with a combined audit log for the full batch.
The Pro GDPR audit log is a detailed JSON or PDF report documenting every PII item redacted, which regulatory article it maps to (GDPR Art. 5(1)(c), CCPA §1798, etc.), the confidence score, and a timestamp. It's designed to be attached to EEOC blind review documentation or presented to your Data Protection Officer as evidence of a data minimisation workflow.
